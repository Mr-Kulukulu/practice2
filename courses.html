Courses Offered

  1. Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSc in CSE)

    Learn programming, web development, databases, artificial intelligence, networking, cybersecurity, and software engineering. Students gain practical skills through projects and laboratory work.

  2. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

    Develop knowledge in business management, accounting, finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, and human resource management to prepare for leadership roles in the corporate world.

  3. Bachelor of Arts in English

    Study English literature, linguistics, communication skills, creative writing, and academic research to build strong language and analytical abilities.

  4. Bachelor of Science in Mathematics

    Explore algebra, calculus, statistics, geometry, probability, and mathematical modeling. The program prepares students for careers in education, research, and data analysis.

  5. Bachelor of Science in Physics

    Learn classical mechanics, electricity and magnetism, quantum physics, thermodynamics, electronics, and experimental physics through classroom and laboratory activities.

  6. Bachelor of Science in Chemistry

    Study organic, inorganic, analytical, and physical chemistry while gaining practical laboratory experience for careers in research and industry.

  7. Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT)

    Focus on computer systems, networking, cloud computing, database management, cybersecurity, and IT support to meet the growing demand for technology professionals.

  8. Bachelor of Accounting

    Learn financial accounting, auditing, taxation, business law, and financial management to prepare for careers in accounting and finance.

  9. Diploma in Software Engineering

    A practical program covering programming, web development, mobile app development, database systems, and software testing.

  10. Diploma in Graphic Design

    Develop skills in digital illustration, branding, UI/UX design, photo editing, typography, and multimedia design using industry-standard software.
