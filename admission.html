Admission Information

Admissions at Greenfield Institute of Technology (GIT) are open for the Spring and Fall semesters. Students who have successfully completed their Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or an equivalent qualification from a recognized education board are eligible to apply.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Completed HSC/A-Level or an equivalent qualification.
  • Minimum GPA of 3.00 (or equivalent).
  • Valid academic certificates and transcripts
  • National ID or Birth Certificate.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs.

Required Documents

  • Completed admission application form.
  • SSC and HSC mark sheets/certificates.
  • National ID or Birth Certificate (photocopy).
  • Two passport-sized photographs.
  • Character certificate from the previous institution.

Admission Process

  1. Fill out the admission application form.
  2. Submit all required documents.
  3. Attend the admission test or interview (if applicable).
  4. Wait for the admission result.
  5. Complete the tuition fee payment.
  6. Receive the student ID card and class schedule after successful enrollment.
