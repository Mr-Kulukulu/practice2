About the College

Greenfield Institute of Technology (GIT) was established in 2005 with the vision of providing quality higher education and preparing students for successful careers. Over the years, the college has earned a reputation for academic excellence, innovation, and student development.

The college offers modern classrooms, advanced computer laboratories, a well-stocked library, science laboratories, sports facilities, and a friendly learning environment. More than 3,500 students are currently enrolled in various undergraduate programs.

Our mission is to develop knowledgeable, responsible, and skilled graduates who can contribute positively to society. We encourage research, creativity, leadership, and lifelong learning.

Vision

To become one of the leading institutions of higher education by promoting academic excellence, innovation, and ethical values.

Mission

  • Provide quality education
  • Encourage research and innovation.
  • Develop leadership and communication skills.
  • Prepare students for successful careers.
  • Serve society through knowledge and community engagement.
